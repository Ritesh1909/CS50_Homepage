Keeping your location in mind, Here are some devices available.
Trainers Edition

Gives rapid information about Pokémons by scanning the body. A perfect setup to search for new Pokémons

Competitive Edition

A more competitive based device to scan the health of your Pokémon. The device scans the Pokéball and gives accurate readings.

Lab Edition

The best research device ever created in the world of Pokémons. Scans the Pokémon and collects all the attributes. All data is given in spreadsheets.
